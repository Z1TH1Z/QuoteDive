## Troubleshooting

### Widget Not Updating?
1. Open the app to trigger an update
2. Check that battery optimization is disabled for Quotes App
3. Remove and re-add the widget

### Widget Shows Old Data?
- The widget refreshes every 30 minutes automatically
- Opening the app forces an immediate update
- Earning points triggers an instant widget refresh

### Can't Find Widget?
- Make sure the app is fully installed
- Restart your device
- Check that you're running Android 5.0 or higher
